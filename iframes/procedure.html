Procedure of Simulator

  1. De-vacuum the chamber
  2. Load the sample
  3. Vacuum the chamber
  4. Wait until the pressure reaches 10-4 Pa
  5. Turn on the beam
  6. Magnify a location
  7. Take an image or start compositional mapping
  8. Turn off the beam
